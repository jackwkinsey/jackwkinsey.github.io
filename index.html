About Me

My name is Jack Kinsey (if you haven't already guessed that), and I am a programmer, developer, and designer. I mostly work on web and game related projects, which you can learn more about here. Also, if you just want to jump to my resume, you can do so here, but stick around and play some of my games or check out my blog. I'm sure I've written about something you'd enjoy.

Anyway, this is the "about" section of this website so if you are still reading this, then I can only assume that you'd like to know a little more about me. Very well. As I mentioned, I am a programmer, which means I love to solve problems. I am a developer, which means I love searching for the best solutions to said problems. And I am a designer, which means I like for those solutions to look pretty.

When I'm not saving the world by making responsive, simple websites, or fun games for the web, I am usually playing bass and synth for my band, Kinetic (check us out here), or catching up on Game of Thrones and Game Grumps. I also enjoy learning about and tasting (but mostly tasting) all different kinds of beer. Currently, my favorite is Boulevard's Rye-on-Rye, and I cannot recommend it enough.

Although I spend most of my time working on various projects, I am still a gamer at heart. My favorite genre of game is the action RPG. (It used to be just RPGs, but I've grown impatient in recent years and now I need lots of action-y goodness. Don't worry I still love a good story.) Currently, I am dying my way through Dark Souls (the first one) and am having a blast doing so. Although I am currently stuck on the Four Kings boss. Yes, I know I need to use Havel's armor and just "swing away," but I just suck, okay?!

Well, that is pretty much it for me. If you have any questions, feel free to ask! You can usually find me on Twitter (@jackwkinsey), so feel free to drop me a line!
